# Business Plan

## Business Thesis

AI coding tools are making software creation accessible to non-technical people, but many learners still need structure, confidence, and practical software literacy. Coding Kitty Studio can become the trusted beginner-friendly bridge between idea and shipped app.

## Revenue Streams

- freemium learning platform
- paid project paths
- templates
- project reviews
- office hours
- cohort programs
- sponsor-backed modules
- hackathon and challenge partnerships
- certificates or badges

## Initial Offer

Free:

- build and deploy your first personal website with AI

Paid:

- build a simple productivity app with an AI coding agent
- deeper modules on version control, deployment, databases, and automation

## Business Questions

- What pricing should the paid path use?
- Should this be subscription, one-time course purchase, or hybrid?
- Should sponsors subsidize free content?
- What would make a learner upgrade after the free path?
