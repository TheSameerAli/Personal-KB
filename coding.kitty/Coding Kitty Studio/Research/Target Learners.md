# Target Learners

## Primary Learners

- non-technical people
- creators
- students
- professionals trying to automate their work

## Learner Jobs To Be Done

- I want to build my idea without waiting for a developer.
- I want to understand what AI coding tools are doing.
- I want to automate repetitive work.
- I want to create a portfolio or personal website.
- I want to build simple apps for myself or my team.
- I want to learn enough coding to feel confident with AI tools.

## Learner Fears

- I do not know where to start.
- I do not understand the code the AI wrote.
- I am afraid I will break something.
- I do not know how to deploy.
- I do not understand GitHub.
- I do not know which tool to use.

## Learner Success

A successful learner can describe an idea, build a first version with AI, save their work with version control, deploy it, and keep improving it.
