# Product Vision

## Vision

Help non-technical people become capable AI-assisted software builders.

The product should be AI-first in the learning experience itself: the learner brings an idea, and coding.kitty/learn turns that idea into a guided, personalized course.

## Core Promise

Go from idea to deployed app using AI coding agents, while learning the software basics needed to stay in control.

Sharper promise:

> Turn your app idea into a personalized AI-building course, then ship it with enough software understanding to own the result.

## Positioning

coding.kitty/learn should feel like a structured learning platform for turning ideas into shipped software, not a traditional coding course.

The strongest positioning is:

> An AI Builder Learning Platform that generates a practical build course from your own app idea.

## What Makes It Different

- learner idea becomes the syllabus
- AI-generated course plan assembled from trusted learning blocks
- project-first learning
- AI-guided development
- practical software fundamentals
- version control as a safety habit
- infrastructure and deployment explained through real projects
- friendly Coding Kitty brand layer without making the product feel unserious

## Product Principles

- Build first, explain when needed.
- Personalize around the learner's idea, not a generic sample app.
- Use AI to assemble and adapt the path, but keep the teaching grounded in curated content.
- Teach learners how to guide AI, not blindly trust it.
- Make every concept connect to a real shipped project.
- Treat version control and deployment as core skills.
- Keep the experience friendly, practical, and confidence-building.
