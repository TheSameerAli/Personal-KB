# Marketing Strategy

## Positioning

Bring your app idea. coding.kitty/learn turns it into a personalized AI-building course and helps you ship it, even if you are starting from zero.

## Core Message

Vibe coding is fun, but shipping real software requires more than vibes. coding.kitty/learn teaches you how to guide AI coding agents, understand what they produce, use version control, deploy your app, and keep improving it.

Short version:

> Your idea becomes the course.

## Launch Wedge

First free experience:

> Describe what you want to build and get a guided AI-building path.

The MVP can still steer beginners toward constrained archetypes such as a personal website or simple productivity app.
