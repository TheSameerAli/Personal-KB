# Brand Strategy

## Brand Relationship

`coding.kitty` remains the media and education brand.

`coding.kitty/learn` becomes the interactive learning platform underneath it.

## Tone

- friendly
- practical
- encouraging
- playful but credible
- beginner-safe

## Theme Level

Medium cat theme.

Use:

- mascot guidance
- playful lesson names
- cat-flavored examples
- badges
- soft visual identity
- short videos in the Coding Kitty tone

Avoid:

- making every concept a joke
- overwhelming the learning interface
- making the product feel unserious for professionals
